Introduction

Data and ideas

The aim of this project is to analyze the international news trends regarding the United States of America in the period 2015-2017. We use the data provided by the The GDELT Project and use their translinugal 2.0 version. It is basically a list of news published online with a few properties for each news such as the Goldstein scale.

In addition to the data provided by GDELT we need the country of the webiste where a news was published, we solve this mostly through web crawling and top level domain matching. Our solution has 77% accuracy, that is 77% of the news get a source country assigned, the 23% left are mostly international website or website not considered an online newspaper, the unassigned news are discarded for this analysis.

The source code is available here: https://github.com/gregunz/ada2017
